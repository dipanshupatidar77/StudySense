 🏢 The Manager’s Dilemma

You’re a team lead in your first major project. One team member always meets deadlines and produces excellent work — but they frequently disrespect colleagues, ignore feedback, and demotivate others. Complaints have reached HR. If you remove them, the project might fall behind. If you don’t, the team morale could collapse.
Reflective Question: As a leader, should performance outweigh team harmony? How do you take a stand without losing control?
